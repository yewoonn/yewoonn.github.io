About Me

I'm currently working as a research intern at the AI Health Informatics Lab, where I contribute to drug response prediction (DRP) studies that support new drug development. My work focuses on interpreting drug–gene relationships at the pathway level, aiming to provide insights into treatment mechanisms and contribute to more targeted and effective therapies. I’m particularly interested in how technology can reduce access barriers for underserved communities. I believe AI has the potential to drive meaningful social impact, and I hope to be part of that change by applying my skills where they are most needed.
